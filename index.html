Our Story

Built on Passion.
Driven by Excellence.

Cataleya was born from a simple belief: every celebration deserves to feel extraordinary, and every job deserves to be done right. As a proud self-owned business based in Port Saint Lucie, FL, we pour our heart into every event we design and every haul we complete.

What started as a passion for creating beautiful spaces has grown into a full-service company trusted by families, businesses, and event planners across South Florida. From intimate backyard gatherings to large-scale corporate events — and everything in between — we show up prepared, creative, and committed.

When you work with us, you're not just hiring a service. You're partnering with someone who genuinely cares about your vision and works tirelessly to bring it to life.
